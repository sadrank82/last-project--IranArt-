Silver cup from Marvdasht, Iran, with a linear-Elamite inscription from the time of Kutik-Inshushinak. National Museum of Iran A silver cup was found in Marvdasht, on which two women, one sitting and the other standing, are engraved, who are wearing clothes made of cloth or plant fibers like leaves, which are comparable to the form of ceremonial clothes of our era.
